## Overview

Learn how to receive a payment with mosambee in under 5 minutes.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open mosambee app">
    ````tip
    Log in to your merchant account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment amount">
    ````tip
    Type the amount and tap "Proceed".
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose payment method">
    ````tip
    Card, UPI, or QR code.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete transaction">
    ````tip
    Hand device to customer for PIN or code.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
