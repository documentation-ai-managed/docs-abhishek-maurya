---
title: Can't Log In to Mosambee App
description: Fix login problems with the mosambee mobile app.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page stays blank
- Shows "Invalid credentials" error

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check network connection">
    ````tip
    Make sure you have internet access.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````bash
    Tap "Forgot Password" and follow instructions.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the app">
    ````tip
    Download latest version from Play Store or App Store.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
